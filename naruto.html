Background

Naruto Uzumaki is the son of the Fourth Hokage, Minato Namikaze, and Kushina Uzumaki. Born with the Nine-Tailed Fox sealed within him, Naruto faced a childhood of loneliness and rejection. Through his unwavering determination and belief in himself, he achieved his dream of becoming Hokage and gained the acknowledgment of everyone in his village.
