Background

Sasuke Uchiha is the last surviving member of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan. After his older brother, Itachi, slaughtered their clan, Sasuke made it his mission to avenge them by killing Itachi. He later discovered the truth about his brother's sacrifice and, after many trials and conflicts, eventually became a powerful ally of Konoha and Naruto's closest friend.
