Background

Kakashi Hatake is a shinobi of the Hidden Leaf Village's Hatake clan. Famed as Kakashi of the Sharingan, he is one of Konoha's most talented ninja, regularly looked to for advice and leadership despite his personal dislike of responsibility. To his students on Team 7, he emphasizes the importance of teamwork.
