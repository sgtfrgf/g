Background

Sakura Haruno started as a young kunoichi with a crush on Sasuke Uchiha and little ninja ability. Through hard work and determination, she became one of the most powerful medical ninja in history, surpassing even her mentor Tsunade. Her incredible chakra control and medical expertise made her an invaluable asset during the Fourth Great Ninja War and beyond.
