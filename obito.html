"In this world, wherever there is light, there are also shadows. As long as the concept of winners exists, there must also be losers. The selfish desire of wanting to maintain peace causes wars, and hatred is born to protect love."

Obito Uchiha was a member of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan. He was believed to have died during the Third Shinobi World War, but survived with the help of Madara Uchiha. Disillusioned by the world's cruelty, he became a key figure in the Akatsuki organization.

Timeline

Early Life

A cheerful ninja who dreamed of becoming Hokage and was deeply in love with his teammate Rin Nohara.

Kannabi Bridge Mission

Nearly died saving Kakashi, was saved by Madara Uchiha, and witnessed Rin's death, leading to his transformation.

Masked Man Period

Operated under the alias "Tobi" and manipulated events from the shadows, including the Nine-Tails' attack on Konoha.

Fourth Great Ninja War

Led the war as the leader of Akatsuki, later redeemed himself and sacrificed his life to save the world.
