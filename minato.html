Background

Minato Namikaze was renowned as the fastest shinobi in history, earning the moniker "Yellow Flash" for his instantaneous Flying Thunder God Technique. As the Fourth Hokage, he sacrificed his life to save Konoha from the Nine-Tails' attack, sealing half of the fox's chakra within his newborn son, Naruto.
